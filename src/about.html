How to play the game?

You can control the camera by clicking and draging with the mouse.
For playing the game :
choose one field of the eight circle and click the circle to begin.
If the movement end, wait for the turn of the second player.


Total number of pawn : 32
player's pawn : 16
opponent's pawn : 16

Win or Loose?

If you have the total number of the pawn, then you are the winner.
Else, if the opponent have the total number of the pawn, you loose.
